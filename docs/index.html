Positioning

What this project is

A practical OpenClaw starter kit for people who want a tighter entry point than “build a full agent platform”. Each skill solves one job clearly enough to demo, test, and iterate.

Why it exists

Why this route makes sense

Early ecosystems reward useful components. Narrow skills are faster to build, easier to explain, and more credible than vague platform promises.

Starter skills

The three-skill chain

Together they form a simple pipeline: research, structure, and recurring output.

01 / research-digest-zh

Research Digest

Turns scattered materials into a source-aware Chinese research brief with findings, implications, and open questions.

  • Best for policy scans and topic research
  • Separates evidence from inference
  • Outputs a decision-oriented digest
sources -> findings -> implications

02 / idea-to-draft-zh

Idea To Draft

Converts a topic or notes into a sharper Chinese first draft by forcing audience, angle, outline, and revision notes.

  • Best for article or note drafting
  • Generates multiple angles before writing
  • Produces a usable first draft, not just brainstorm
topic -> angle -> outline -> draft

03 / source-to-weekly-brief-zh

Weekly Brief

Converts a week's source updates into a compact Chinese brief focused on what changed, why it matters, and what to do next.

  • Best for recurring ecosystem tracking
  • Prioritizes deltas over repetition
  • Ends with concrete action items
weekly inputs -> delta summary -> next actions
